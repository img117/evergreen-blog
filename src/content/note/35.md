CCU is a network gateway. It's responsible for connecting the entire vehicle to the outside world (e.g. cloud, server, telematics center, etc...).

Why does the vehicle need communication with a server?
- 교통 정보 요청
- 원격 제어 (remote door lock, air conditioning, etc...)
- OTA (Over-the-Air) Update
- 실시간 경로 요청

The external data must go through CCU to be distributed to other devices in vehicle ([DCU act as an internal data hub](/note/36)).

But note that this specification isn't always the case. Depending on the OEM/variant structure, other component may take over CCU's task and vice versa.
